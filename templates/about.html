About URL Shortener

The URL Shortener is a simple tool that helps you shorten long URLs into shorter, more manageable links. It's designed to make sharing and distributing URLs easier.

With the URL Shortener, you can create customized, concise links that redirect to the original long URLs. It's useful for social media sharing, email marketing campaigns, and any other situation where you want to save space and make your links more presentable.

The URL Shortener also tracks the number of clicks on each shortened link, providing you with valuable insights into the popularity and reach of your shared URLs.
